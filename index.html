About

Hack o Relay 3.0 is a 48 hours hackathon organized annually by GDSC at Dronacharya College of Engineering. This year it's back for the third time with more excitement, challenges and prizes!

This would be the perfect platform for to connect, learn and grow. It's time get your hands dirty and solve some real world problems. Are your ready for a memorable adventure?

FAQ

The competition is open to all students affiliated with any accredited college or university at the date on which they enter the competition.

Please bring a valid university ID, a laptop, chargers, and any hardware you will use for your hack.

We allow teams having up to 4 people to participate in the hackathon. Only the team lead needs to sign up individually and specify the other team members on the registration form.

The Hackathon is free for everyone, you do not need to pay any amount.

The Event venue is Dronacharya College of Engineering, KHENTAWAS, FARRUKH NAGAR, Gurugram, Haryana 123506. You can reach by public transports like Train (Nearest Railway Station: Paatli), Bus, etc. as per your convenience.

Please contact us at +91-8278050139 if you’re in any way confused or concerned! We’d love to help you out!
